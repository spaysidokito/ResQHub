Earthquake Safety Tips (Offline)

  • DROP - Get down on your hands and knees
  • COVER - Take cover under a sturdy desk or table
  • HOLD ON - Hold on until shaking stops
  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects
  • Be prepared for aftershocks
  • Have an emergency kit ready with water, food, and supplies
  • Know your evacuation routes
